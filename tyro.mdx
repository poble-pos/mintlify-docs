## Prerequisites

Before you begin, please ensure you have the following:

- A **Tyro EFTPOS Terminal**
- An active **Tyro Merchant ID**
- Access to **POBLE POS**
- Internet access for terminal communication

---

## Step-by-Step Integration

### 1. On the Tyro EFTPOS Terminal

- Press the **Menu** button.
- Select **Configuration**, then enter your admin password.

- Select **Integrated EFTPOS**.
- Select **Pair with POS**.

### 2. In the POBLE POS

- Navigate to **Settings > Payment Settings > Tyro**.
- Toggle on **Pair Tyro Terminal**.
- Enter your **Merchant ID** and **Terminal ID**, then click **Pair**.

### 3. Start Pairing

- On the EFTPOS terminal, press **Start** to initiate the pairing process.

---

## Integrated Receipts

To print EFTPOS receipts from your POS receipt printer, enable the **Integrated Receipts** option within the payment settings.

---

## Integrated Surcharges

To allow Tyro to apply card surcharges automatically, enable the **Integrated Surcharge** option in your payment configuration.
